📚 Rules reference

Definite vs Indefinite
‘the’ = specific; ‘a/an’ = non-specific, first mention.
First vs subsequent mention
First: ‘a/an’. Later: ‘the’.
Countable vs uncountable
Countable singular needs article; uncountable can be zero article.
Geographic names
Rivers, seas, plural countries → ‘the’. Single mountains, lakes → zero.
Proper nouns
Most proper nouns → zero article. Exceptions: the UK, the USA.
General statements
Plural/uncountable → zero article: “Dogs are loyal.”
