📖 When tense shifts ARE appropriate

✅ Reported speech — “She said she is tired.” (past reporting + present state)
✅ General truth in past narrative — “The teacher explained that water boils at 100°C.”
✅ Change of time frame — “He lived in Paris, but now he lives in London.”

❓ FAQ

When is a shift flagged? If a sentence uses a tense different from the paragraph’s dominant tense, and the shift is not a standard exception (reported speech, general truth). The tool marks it as a potential unnecessary shift.
